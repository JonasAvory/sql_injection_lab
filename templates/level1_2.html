Level 1.2 — Login Bypass (password checked first)

The WHERE clause is password = '...' AND username = '...' — the password is compared before the username. So admin' -- only comments out what comes after the username, leaving the password check active, and it fails. You must use OR instead. But note OR '1'='1 matches every row, so the app just returns the first one (not necessarily the account you want). To target a specific account, scope the OR to it, e.g. x' OR username='admin.
